KTM is an Austrian motorcycle brand renowned for its high-performance off-road and street motorcycles, particularly in motocross, enduro, and adventure segments. Known for its signature orange color and aggressive design, KTM bikes combine advanced technology with a focus on lightweight and powerful engines. The brand excels in racing, dominating events like the Dakar Rally and MotoGP. KTM is celebrated for its Ready to Race philosophy, emphasizing performance and agility. With a strong global presence, KTM caters to both professional racers and adventure enthusiasts seeking thrilling riding experiences.
